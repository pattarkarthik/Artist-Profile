About

Karthik Pattar is a Bangalore based stand up comedian who has done about 300 shows all around the globe. He started doing comedy on stage from his childhood days where he used to do comedy dramas, mimicry etc., He is the winner of state level mimicry competition held by karnataka state education board, he was part of zee kannada's first edition of comedy Kiladigalu as a child artist. During his college days he was part of college radicle mime team as a Narrator and actor, and we have won more than 50 intercollegiate fests.

Having more than 10 years of experience in Kannada standup comedy Karthik Pattar is also a Ventriloquist and an actor, he has acted in 4 kannada feature films and also has written scripts for two films recently he did a show called "The Cricket Showman in Start Sports Kannada as part of IPL
